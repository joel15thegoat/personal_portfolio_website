About Me

Practical security engineering backed by leadership and resilience.

I focus on defensive security, system monitoring, and applied cryptography. As a 3rd Sergeant in NCC and a Young Engineer Award recipient, I bring teamwork, discipline, and practical problem solving.

Read more about my journey

Since I was young, I've been drawn to the world of technology – especially hacking. A YouTube clip from the show Mr. Robot, where Elliot exposes a criminal through hacking, first sparked my interest in coding. It showed not only how hackers can use social engineering, but also the real dangers of illegal hacking.

In my free time I dive into the history of cyber conflict: the first cyberattack between Russia and Estonia, the Shadow Brokers leaking zero‑day exploits from the "Equation Group", and many more. These stories deepened my passion for defensive security and ethical hacking – and they continue to drive my self‑taught journey in building practical, secure tools.
